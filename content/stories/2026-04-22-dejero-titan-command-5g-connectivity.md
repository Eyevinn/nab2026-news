[Dejero used NAB 2026](https://www.dejero.com/blog/dejero-unveils-titan-family-revolutionary-mission-critical-connectivity-portfolio-launches-with-titan-command/) (booth W2761) to stage a live "Field to Air" demonstration across multiple booths on the show floor, with Eutelsat, Clear-Com, GlobalM, Matrox Video, and Ross Video all connected through TITAN Command in real time.

TITAN Command is the flagship product of Dejero's new TITAN family, built around a triple 5G modem architecture with 14 integrated antennas, GPS/GNSS, and Smart Blending Technology that performs packet-level optimization across multiple simultaneous network paths. The hardware fits in a laptop-sized chassis rated for operation from -40°C to 70°C, and holds FirstNet, Verizon Frontline, and FedRAMP Moderate (in-process) certifications for mission-critical environments. The roadmap behind it includes TITAN Mobile in Q2 2026 for vehicle-optimized deployment, TITAN Scout in Q3 for portable battery-powered field use, TITAN Ghost in Q4 as a virtualized variant, and TITAN Control for unified management across the family.

Matt Scully, Director of Product Management at Dejero, described TITAN Command as "a quantum leap in mission-critical connectivity previously impossible in a single device." TITAN Command also won the TV Technology Best of Show Award at NAB 2026. The multi-partner live demo format — spanning five companies across physical booth locations — served as a functional proof of the bonding architecture under real show-floor network conditions.
