[Dalet is announcing commercial availability of Dalia at NAB Show 2026](https://dalet.com/news/dalia-agentic-ai-end-to-end-media-workflows-nab-show-2026/) (Booth W1519), its media-aware agentic AI solution. Dalia uses a natural-language conversational interface combined with a multi-agent orchestration framework to translate user intent into executable media workflow sequences. A single request can trigger asset discovery, clip creation, review, and publishing in sequence without the user touching individual tools.

Early deployments show measurable reductions in time spent on content discovery and production tasks, though Dalet has not published specific benchmark figures. The commercial release marks the product's transition from early access to a generally available offering for enterprise broadcast and media customers.

The positioning is notable for its audience scope. Dalet is explicitly targeting sales, marketing, and brand teams alongside traditional broadcast operations — groups that are now producing and managing content at volume but may lack dedicated media operations staff.

"Dalet is bringing the power of enterprise media workflows to a much broader audience, from traditional broadcasters to sales, marketing, and brand teams that are now producing and managing content at scale," said Matteo De Martinis, EVP of Product Management.
