[TV Technology announced more than 80 Best of Show Award winners](https://www.tvtechnology.com/events/tv-tech-announces-winners-of-best-of-show-awards-at-2026-nab-show) from the 2026 NAB Show floor, providing the broadest formal industry snapshot of products that drew recognition across the show's final days.

Notable recipients span the full spectrum of broadcast infrastructure: AJA BRIDGE LIVE IP, AWS Elemental Inference, Avid Content Core, Canon CINE-SERVO 40-1200mm, Calrec ImPulseV, Dalet Dalia, Dejero TITAN Command, Evertz X-CALIBER, Haivision Makito ONE, Harmonic Spectrum X Plus, Lawo Edge One, LiveU LU900Q, OWC Express 4M2 Ultra (Future category), Riedel MediorNet HorizoN ST 2110 Multiviewer App, Ross Video XPression Tessera, Shure DCA901, Signiant Connected Intelligence, Sony XYN Spatial Suite, and Zixi Market Switching, among many others.

The breadth of AI-branded products across the winner list — from encoding and monitoring to newsroom automation and storage orchestration — illustrates how thoroughly AI positioning has saturated every product category present on the show floor. The Future category, won by OWC's Thunderbolt 5 enclosure, recognizes products not yet shipping. A parallel set of 15 AV Technology sister awards added Vizrt CaptivAIte and the Wowza Video Intelligence Framework to the recognized list.
