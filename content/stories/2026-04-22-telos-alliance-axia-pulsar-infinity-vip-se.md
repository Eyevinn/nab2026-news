[Telos Alliance used NAB 2026 to debut three new products](https://www.mixonline.com/technology/news-products/telos-alliance-launches-four-new-products-at-nab-2026) alongside its previously announced Omnia XII FM processor: the Axia Pulsar console surface, the Telos Infinity VIP SE, and the Telos Infinity IP Intercom MK2.

The Axia Pulsar is a compact Audio over IP console surface with eight motorized faders expandable to 16, paired with a touchscreen monitor section. It is designed for broadcast facilities that want AoIP flexibility without the footprint of larger console surfaces. The Telos Infinity VIP SE merges Infinity VIP intercom software with a compact hardware form factor intended for productions that need a smaller physical panel count. The Infinity IP Intercom MK2 upgrades its Master, Expansion, and Desktop panels with 16 larger, brighter TFT button displays to improve on-air readability in demanding lighting environments.

Telos also demonstrated the Jünger Audio flexAI platform with a new cloud-deployable AI mixing assistant called JAIC, and the Studio Zero fully virtual cloud-hosted broadcast studio ecosystem. The combination of hardware panel refreshes and cloud-native audio tooling reflects a pattern visible across the NAB floor this year: vendors maintaining physical product lines while simultaneously building parallel software and cloud offerings for production environments moving away from fixed infrastructure.
