- **Summary**: Streaming Summit chair Dan Rayburn opened the two-day event on April 20 in the West Hall with a pointed critique of AI-generated misinformation spreading through the media industry. He estimated approximately 75% of industry information shared on LinkedIn is inaccurate, and gave concrete examples including ChatGPT falsely attributing Peacock Super Bowl commentary to him and Google Gemini recommending an Apple Mail app discontinued 12 years ago. He called on attendees to stop sharing unvetted information and praised AI's legitimate uses, such as the PGA Tour's pipeline that creates 7,000 videos daily.
- **Quote**: "Stop sharing information you haven't vetted...take the time to actually see if the information is accurate."
