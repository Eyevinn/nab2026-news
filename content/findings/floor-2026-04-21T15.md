- **Topic tags**: broadcast, production, ip-video
- **Image**:
- **Image alt**:
- **Image credit**:
- **Summary**: Neutrik announced the True1 DATA connector series at NAB 2026, bringing its rugged True1 locking form factor to data connectivity. The family includes three variants: mediaCON True1 (USB-C with USB 3.2 Gen 1×1 at 5 Gbps and USB Power Delivery 3.0 up to 100W), etherCON True1 (RJ45 with CAT6A performance supporting 10 Gigabit Ethernet), and opticalCON True1 (two-channel fiber optic in multi-mode and single-mode versions).
